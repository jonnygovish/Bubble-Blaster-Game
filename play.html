What it Bubble Blaster?

Bubble blaster is a fun game where a player pops bubbles. When a player clicks 'Start', bubbles of different sizes rise up from the bottom of the page at different speeds.

Each game is allocated a 30 second span within which you, the player have to pop as many bubbles as you can. When time runs out, the bubbles stop rising and your score is displayed. You can then choose to start a new game.

Enjoy!!!
